  2. Extract it to a trusted folder (Desktop or a tools folder).
  3. Whitelist the folder in your antivirus to prevent false flags.
  4. Start your game, then open the executor.
  5. Press Attach, wait for the status to show ‚ÄúConnected‚Äù.
  6. Paste your script, press Execute.
